My First Post

January 1, 2025

Welcome to my first post. This is where I share how I got started with minimalist personal websites and why I think a light, uncluttered design is the best approach for a personal site.

Instead of adding more and more features, I like to focus on the core reading experience: good typography, calm colors, and a layout that doesn't get in the way. This site is my small experiment in that direction.

Over time, I'll write more about color choices, spacing, and small UX decisions that make a site feel simple without feeling empty.
