Another Minimalist Update

January 5, 2025

Another quick snippet on this minimalist site. Here I share why I prefer lighter color palettes, even when the overall theme leans dark.

Light backgrounds with plenty of contrast keep the focus on reading. They also make small visual details—like headings and links—feel calmer instead of loud.

As I keep iterating on this site, I’ll keep testing small shifts in color and spacing, but the goal stays the same: a quiet reading experience that feels modern without feeling busy.
